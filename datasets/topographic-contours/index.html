Water Department

Water Department, City of Philadelphia

Topographic Contours

Topographic contours, developed by the City of Philadelphia Water Department based on aerial survey performed in 1996. Annotation of Contour Line elevations exists. Annotation viewable at 1" = 200'. The city-wide contour download file is approximately 250 megabytes.
