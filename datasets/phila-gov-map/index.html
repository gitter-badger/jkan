Office of Innovation & Technology, City of Philadelphia

Phila.gov Map

Data from the Department of License and Inspections, Planning, Parks and Recreation, Heath, etc, can be viewed on this interactive map. Preset maps can be selected containing datasets from the following categories: Bike Network, Business, Construction, Gun Permit Appeals, Rental Licenses, Vacancy, Violations, and Zoning. Users can also overlay districts on the map, such as Census Tracts or Sanitation Districts. Additional datasets will be added over time. API access is available through the ArcGIS Server Map Services API interface.
