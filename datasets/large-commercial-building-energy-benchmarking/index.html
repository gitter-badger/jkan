Office of Sustainability, City of Philadelphia

Large Commercial Building Energy Benchmarking

This dataset contains building and performance data for those properties which reported during Year Two and Year Three of the energy benchmarking and disclosure program, which covered the 2013 and 2014 calendar years. Property data is pulled from the Office of Property Assessment. Data is self-reported by building owners using the EPA Portfolio Manager tool. This data will be updated annually.
