City Commissioners

City Commissioners, City of Philadelphia

8 datasets

Analyze the Vote

Election data from 2007 through 2014 can be analyzed and displayed on a map, scatterplot, or in a tabular format. The site allows users to compare election results by candidate, election year, office, as well as many other facets. The raw election data can also be downloaded in text format.

Candidates

The individuals who are candidates for elected office. Internally, this information is used to create the ballot. Externally, candidates and campaigns will want to know who their opponents are.

Elected Committeepeople

This data set is a list of elected Committee People by ward, division, and party.

Election Board Who Worked on Election Day

This data reflects which Election Board Officials worked on Election Day and received payment for their services.

Polling Places

An API containing the locations of polling places in Philadelphia, along with parking code and building accessibility code attribute markers. Data is originally provided by the Philadelphia City Commissioners and is continuously updated in correspondence with the City Commissioners website, www.p...

Election Results

Data for both primary and general elections, 2007 - 2004. Includes separate files for initial results and certified results.

Qualified Voter Registry

Information regarding individuals who are registered to vote. This data is broken down into various reports for voter, election, and district information.

Voter Turnout

Information regarding individuals who have voted and when. This data is broken down into various reports for voter, election, and district information.
